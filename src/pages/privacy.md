At DaemonLayer, we value your privacy and take data protection seriously.  
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our automation platform.

By using DaemonLayer, you consent to the practices described below.

## 1. Information We Collect

1.1. **Account Information** — Name, email address, and company details when you register for access.

1.2. **Usage Data** — Logs of activity, integrations, and system interactions to maintain and improve the platform.

1.3. **Support Data** — Information related to tickets or automations processed through your PSA integration.

1.4. **Cookies and Analytics** — We use cookies to enhance functionality and measure site performance.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

2.1. To deliver automation workflows, integrations, and analytics features.

2.2. To communicate important updates, security alerts, and product information.

2.3. To improve service performance and support reliability.

2.4. Aggregated, anonymized data may be used for internal metrics and platform optimization.

## 3. Data Protection and Storage

3.1. All data is hosted in secure, EU-based cloud environments.

3.2. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Each MSP operates in a logically isolated environment.

3.3. No ticket data is stored beyond the processing required for automation, and none is used to train third-party AI models.

## 4. Data Access and Sharing

4.1. We do not sell or rent customer data.

4.2. Limited data may be shared with trusted subprocessors (e.g., infrastructure providers) bound by confidentiality and GDPR-compliant agreements.

4.3. We may disclose information if required by law or legal obligation.

## 5. Security and Compliance

5.1. We adhere to GDPR principles and follow ISO 27001-aligned best practices.

5.2. Access to data is restricted through role-based controls and continuous monitoring.

5.3. Security incidents, if any, are handled under strict response procedures.

## 6. Your Rights

6.1. You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.

6.2. Requests can be made by contacting **privacy@daemonlayer.com**.

## 7. Updates to this Policy

7.1. We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The revised version will be posted on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.

## 8. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or our data handling practices, contact us at **privacy@daemonlayer.com**.
